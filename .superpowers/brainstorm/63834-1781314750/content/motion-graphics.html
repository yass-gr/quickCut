Key Motion Graphics Principles

Morph transitions

No hard cuts — elements dissolve, reform, or stretch into the next scene

Particle assembly

Logos and text assemble from particles rather than sliding in from off-screen

Spring physics

Bounce, overshoot, settle — not linear or ease-out

Parallax stacking

Elements at different depths move at different speeds (z-space feel)

Gradient sweeps

Background gradients animate (team colors sweep across frame)

Motion blur

Fast-moving elements get directional blur for fluid feel
